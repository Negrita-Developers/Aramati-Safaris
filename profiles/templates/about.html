  • Welcome to Kenya -- "Karibuni Kenya"

Kenya Sharing Safaris was established to give to our clients the opportunity to save money by sharing tours with other travellers without compromising the same luxury service experience of a private tour. We realized that solo or two travelers find it hard to get a safari that best suits their budget, time and schedule. We are here to offer unique joining safari experiences at an affordable budget for all.

In this website, our guests can either create a group on their own or join a group already open. The site is easy to use and saves time.  All you need to do is simply select the date and join a group or create your own. Once you have completed the booking process, you will receive an automated confirmation voucher and can then invite other passengers to join you or see other people in the group. If you would like more information regarding a specific group, we invite you to contact the group creator via the link provided for that group.  For private tours kindly contact us directly.

Our experienced driver/guides look forward to providing you with a memorable shared safari in Kenya!
