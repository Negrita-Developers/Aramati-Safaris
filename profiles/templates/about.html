Aramati Safaris is a tour agency that serves students and church groups traveling throughout East Africa.

We offer professional, friendly drivers with an excellent knowledge of Kenya wildlife, safe and secure accommodations at all levels (backpackers to luxury tourists), quick and thorough personal service (that's our important part in your visit), and prices that will surprise you.

Could you email us about your safari? You will reach us at info@aramatisafaris.co.ke, at this phone (+ 254 722 443 274) and at this skype Id: Aramati Safaris. Our clients will tell you, that we love to talk about East Africa, the people, game parks, villages and cities. At your request, we will provide names and contacts of visitors who have visited Kenya many times with Aramati (which means, by the way, "stewardship" in my mother tongue, Gikuyu.) These are campus leaders and professors, church leaders and pastors, and personal travelers. Let them tell you about Aramati's dedication to service and safety, and our personal dedication to your visit.
